### Writing Style & Tone

**Overall Tone:** Premium, aspirational, descriptive

**Key Characteristics:**
1. **Descriptive Adjectives:** "Immaculate", "beautifully finished", "elegant", "stylish", "generous", "spacious", "sleek"
2. **Lifestyle Language:** "ideal for professionals, first-time buyers, or investors alike"
3. **Feature-Focused:** Detailed descriptions of finishes, materials, and built-in features
4. **Location Context:** Specific distances to amenities, transport links
5. **Narrative Flow:** Reads like a guided tour through the property

**Sample Phrases:**
- "Immaculate one bedroom flat with lovely balcony in the heart of Wandsworth"
- "beautifully finished one-bedroom apartment offers immaculate interiors"
- "Cleverly designed for modern living"
- "elegantly finished with a full-width wall mirror, overhead rainfall shower"
- "Floor-to-ceiling glass doors open onto a substantial private balcony"

**Structure Pattern:**
1. Opening sentence: Property type + key feature + location
2. Overview paragraph: Overall appeal, target audience, main selling points
3. Reception/Living areas: Layout, flooring, lighting, flow
4. Kitchen: Finishes, appliances, space
5. Bedrooms: Size, features, storage
6. Bathrooms: Finishes, fixtures
7. Outdoor space (if applicable)
8. Location benefits: Transport, amenities, distances

### Photo Guidelines

**Photo Count:** 7-10 high-quality images

**Photo Types:**
1. **Hero Image (Cover):** Best interior shot showing living space
2. **Living/Reception Room:** Multiple angles
3. **Kitchen:** Show appliances and countertops
4. **Bedroom(s):** Natural light, space perception
5. **Bathroom:** Clean, well-lit
6. **Outdoor Space:** Balcony, garden, or terrace
7. **Exterior:** Building exterior and entrance

**Photo Style:**
- Professional photography
- Wide-angle shots
- Bright, natural lighting
- Staged with tasteful decor
- No people visible
- High resolution

### Consistent Branding Elements

**Must-Have on Every Page:**
1. ✅ Savills logo in yellow box (top right)
2. ✅ Property headline (same on every page)
3. ✅ Address
4. ✅ Bed/Bath/Reception icons
5. ✅ Guide Price
6. ✅ Warm beige background color

**Footer Elements (Last Page):**
1. ✅ Agent contact details
2. ✅ Property reference numbers
3. ✅ "powered by FluxPro" logo
4. ✅ Legal disclaimer
5. ✅ "Viewing strictly by appointment"

### Photo Guidelines (PREMIUM)

**Photo Count:** 10+ high-quality images

**Photo Emphasis:**
1. **Lifestyle/Artistic:** More emphasis on atmosphere, light, styling
2. **Exterior Context:** Building exterior, canal views, neighborhood character
3. **Fewer Room-by-Room:** More overview shots vs detailed room photos

### Pattern Recognition: Template Selection

**STANDARD Template (Brochure 1):**
- Entry to mid-range properties (£200k-£800k)
- Detailed descriptions (400-600 words)
- Room-by-room breakdown
- Full bed/bath/reception icons on every page
- 6 pages typical

**PREMIUM Template (Brochure 2):**
- High-value properties (£1M+)
- Minimal text (150-200 words)
- Lifestyle/aspirational focus
- More photos, less copy
- 10 pages typical
- Contact info on page 2 (not last page)
