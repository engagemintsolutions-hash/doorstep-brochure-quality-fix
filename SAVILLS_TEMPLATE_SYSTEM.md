# Savills Template Selection System
**Based on analysis of 4+ sample brochures**

---

## Template Types Identified

### 1. STANDARD Template
**Used for:** Traditional properties, family homes, standard apartments

**Characteristics:**
- 6 pages typical
- Detailed room-by-room descriptions (300-500 words)
- Bed/bath/reception icons on every page header
- Header strip with headline on ALL pages
- "About this property" section with full paragraphs
- Floor plan with detailed measurements
- Key Information page with full details
- Contact info on LAST page only

**Examples:**
- ¬£425k, 1-bed flat (Drapers Yard) - 6 pages
- ¬£930k, 1-bed flat (The Courthouse) - 6 pages
- ¬£1.1M, 4-bed house (Tolverne Road) - 6 pages

**Page Structure:**
1. Cover: Full-bleed hero image + header strip with all info
2. Photo gallery: 6-8 photos in grid layout
3. Description: "About this property" + photos on right
4. Floor plan + "Local Area" description
5. Key Information: Council tax, tenure, EPC, services
6. Contact + More Information + Legal disclaimer

---

### 2. PREMIUM Template
**Used for:** Unique properties, loft-style apartments, character properties, lifestyle properties

**Characteristics:**
- 10 pages typical
- Minimal text (150-200 words total)
- Text OVERLAID on cover photo (white text)
- Contact info on PAGE 2 (not last page)
- NO bed/bath/reception icons on cover
- NO detailed header strip on every page
- Lifestyle/aspirational photography emphasis
- More photos, less copy
- "Location" as dedicated section vs "Local Area"

**Examples:**
- ¬£1.65M, loft apartment (Shepherdess Walk) - 10 pages

**Page Structure:**
1. Cover: Full-bleed photo with white text overlay
2. Summary: Large exterior photo + short intro + CONTACT INFO
3-6. Photo spreads with minimal text (3-4 sentences per page)
7. Location: Dedicated page with large photos
8. Floor plan: Full page
9. Key Information: Minimal layout
10. Contact + Legal

## Writing Style Guidelines

### STANDARD Template Tone
**Descriptive, detailed, informative**

**Characteristics:**
- Room-by-room walkthrough
- Technical details (finishes, appliances, materials)
- Specific measurements and dimensions
- Transport links with distances
- Amenities with walking times
- Target audience mentions

**Example phrases:**
- "Immaculate one bedroom flat with lovely balcony"
- "beautifully finished", "elegantly designed"
- "features sleek wooden flooring and abundant natural light"
- "Floor-to-ceiling glass doors open onto a substantial private balcony"
- "Located within the vibrant [area] development"
- "ideal for professionals, first-time buyers, or investors alike"

**Structure:**
1. Opening: Property type + key feature + location
2. Reception/living: Layout, flooring, lighting, flow
3. Kitchen: Finishes, appliances, space, features
4. Bedrooms: Size, storage, views
5. Bathrooms: Fixtures, finishes
6. Outdoor space: Balcony, garden, terrace
7. Location benefits: Transport, amenities, lifestyle

### PREMIUM Template Tone
**Aspirational, minimal, lifestyle-focused**

**Characteristics:**
- Short, punchy sentences
- Emphasis on unique selling points
- Lifestyle language over technical specs
- Views and ambiance over room dimensions
- "Artfully", "bespoke", "thoughtfully"
- Less "what" more "feel"

**Example phrases:**
- "A truly unique loft style apartment"
- "artfully designed and thoughtfully presented"
- "The views from the apartment are stunning"
- "bespoke luxury kitchen fitted with high quality units"
- "artist commissioned d√©cor"
- "exceptional high standard"

**Structure:**
1. Opening: Unique selling proposition
2. Overall impression: High-level features
3. Standout features: What makes it special
4. Lifestyle benefits: Views, ambiance, experience
5. Location context: Neighborhood character

## Photo Guidelines

### STANDARD Template Photos
**Count:** 7-10 images

**Types Required:**
1. Hero image (cover): Best interior shot, wide angle
2. Living/reception room: 2-3 angles
3. Kitchen: Appliances and workspace visible
4. Each bedroom: Natural light emphasis
5. Main bathroom: Clean, well-lit
6. Outdoor space (if applicable)
7. Building exterior

**Style:**
- Professional photography
- Wide-angle shots
- Well-staged with tasteful decor
- Bright, natural lighting
- No people visible

### PREMIUM Template Photos
**Count:** 10-15 images

**Types Required:**
1. Hero image (cover): Most dramatic shot
2. Exterior + context: Building and neighborhood
3. Overview shots: Multiple angles of main spaces
4. Detail shots: Unique features, finishes
5. View shots: What you see from windows
6. Lifestyle shots: Terrace, balcony, outdoor spaces
7. Neighborhood: Street scenes, local character

**Style:**
- Artistic/editorial quality
- Emphasis on light and atmosphere
- More exterior/context shots
- Show the "experience" not just rooms
- Can include architectural details

## Floor Plan Standards

### Color Coding (Both Templates)
- **Pink/Peach (#F8D7DA):** Reception rooms, living areas, dining, kitchen
- **Purple (#D8BFD8):** Bedrooms
- **Blue (#B0D4E3):** Bathrooms, utility rooms
- **White/Gray:** Hallways, balconies, terraces
- **Black (#000000):** Walls, structural elements

### Required Information
1. Property name and address
2. "Approximate Gross Internal Area" with sq ft and sq m
3. Terrace/balcony area (if applicable)
4. Room dimensions (metric and imperial)
5. Floor label ("Ground floor", "First Floor", etc.)
6. Compass direction indicator
7. Provider credit ("¬© Click Media Group", "¬© ollyhewitt.co.uk", etc.)
8. RICS disclaimer text

### Layout
- Full page or half page
- North arrow always visible
- Clear room labels
- Doors and windows marked
- Ceiling height markers where relevant

## EPC (Energy Performance Certificate) Display

### Visual Format
- Always shown as colored horizontal bar chart
- A-G rating scale with colors:
  - **A (92+):** Dark green
  - **B (81-91):** Light green
  - **C (69-80):** Yellow-green
  - **D (55-68):** Yellow
  - **E (39-54):** Orange
  - **F (21-38):** Dark orange
  - **G (1-20):** Red

- Show both "Current" and "Potential" ratings
- Include numeric score
- Label: "Energy Efficiency Rating"
- Footer: "England, Scotland & Wales" + EU Directive reference

### Placement
- Always on "Key Information" page
- Right column, top position
- Above or alongside services information

## Key Information Page Format

### Left Column
**Local Authority**
- Borough or council name

**Council Tax**
- Band letter (A-H)

**Tenure**
- Freehold / Leasehold
- If leasehold: Lease expiry year

**Ground Rent** (if applicable)
- Amount per annum
- Review frequency

**Service Charge** (if applicable)
- Amount per annum
- Review frequency

### Middle Column
**Services & Additional Information**
- List of included services
- Parking details
- Building amenities
- Special features
- Important notes or warnings

### Right Column
**EPC**
- Visual EPC chart
- Current rating
- Potential rating
